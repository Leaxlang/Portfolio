Project Overview

As the Web Manager for the FY 24 board of the Cal State LA chapter of the Society of Women Engineers, I've led the development of a robust WordPress-based website. This platform is strategically crafted to bolster our online presence, facilitate membership growth, and effectively communicate our mission and values.

Development Process

1. Plan

Our journey began with a comprehensive planning phase. We defined our goals, identified our target audience, and outlined the website's structure and key features. This step was crucial in ensuring that the website aligns with our organization's mission and serves our members and community effectively.

2. Design

With a clear plan it was easy to start the design phase, in which I incorporated the ideas the board had made. We focused on making the website SWE branded, and easily accessible.
